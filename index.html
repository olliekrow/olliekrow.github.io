About

Building with intention — across disciplines.

I'm a Program Manager who works at the intersection of structured systems and creative work. By day I build the workflows, tooling, and documentation that help teams ship. By night I design games and write.

My PM work is rooted in lean, native tooling — getting the most out of platforms teams already use without introducing tool sprawl. I prototype in a demo space before proposing org-level changes, because nobody likes a surprise restructure.
